Our History

Founded in 2015, The Camera Club was started by a group of photographers with the common interest of setting up a camera store. Over the years, we've grown into a large community, now offering a curated selection of high-quality cameras, lenses, and accessories.

As photographers, we understand the frustration that can come from bad workmanship, long lead times and poor communication. That's why we've expanded our services to include camera repairs, trade-ins, and expert consultations. Our commitment to providing exceptional service and guidance will not change.

Today, we remain dedicated to helping photographers — both beginners and professionals — capture moments with the finest gear and support.
